### Redirecting stdin/stdout/stderr

* stdin - what you type into terminal
* stdout - terminal screen
* stderr - also goes to terminal screen
* Redirecting stdin: `<`
* Redirecting stdout:
    * `>` overwrite
    * `>>` appends
* Redirecting stderr: `2>`
